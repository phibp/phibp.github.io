April 22, 2023

a couple days ago, i woke up. not that surprising, really.

however, when i booted up my computer that morning, i opened up the word document containing the book that i have been writing for a few years now, and-

POOF!

~a blank document~

checking the file, it quite literally contained ZERO BYTES inside. it was now just a pointer to a file that didnt exist.

oh, but how could this have happened? i wasnt sure at first, but i immediately blamed myself, since the file was stored on a server that i had set up. though, it quite literally happened overnight, and i couldnt have done anything, so it must have just been a case of...*gasp*

a normal person kind of computer problem?

no way... unheard of, at least with me. well, at least i could blame it on radiation or whatever the hell, and breathe a sigh of relief, knowing i am completely innocent... until i remember that all of my precious work was now GONE.

"well..." i thought to myself. "i-i-i havent actually written anything for a while (since i finished the writing part and was now doing some artwork for it), s-so i should have a copy without any pictures yet at my mothers place? r-right?"

whilst the answer was most likely YES, i wasnt gonna be going there for another couple days, so the thought that everything was well and truly gone definitely haunted me for at least a moment longer. in preparation for the worst, i was getting ready to have to write it all again, and also thinking of better backup strategies, of course.

fortunately, it didnt come to that, and my mother ended up having a copy. still gonna back everything up though, as im even more dead if the same were to happen to my music instead.

annoyingly though, the copy my mother had was in PDF format, which was gonna be a doozy.

i highlighted the entire thing and copied it into a new word document. all was fine, but there was one glaring issue, which took me a good second to notice.

every line was treated as if it were a seperate paragraph. heres a recreation:

you see, instead of just rolling over to the next line with a small gap, anything that went past

the border of the page is moved down as if you pressed "enter". notice how HORRIBLE it looks?

oh and did i mention that any italic or bold text was lost in the copy-paste? yeah. i was also

was dumb enough not to think of just using a converter... it was way too late when i realised-

whatever- everythings back to normal, now, and i even finished the artwork! though, uh- if you want me to upload the book here, uhm... too bad~ :3

well, yeah. thats my story. love ya, unnamed 5 readers! <3
